易用性健檢：全家支付

全支付（My FamiPay）是專屬全家便利商店的行動支付服務。
*這篇易用性健診報告僅為參考範例，並非我在Google Play的工作成果。

內容大綱：

  • 審查原則
  • 問題與改善建議
  • 設計建議

我的易用性檢驗原則

可見度

使用者能看到並輕鬆閱讀所有重要資訊。

理解度

使用者可以清楚理解所有資訊

控制與操作
所有互動元件都運作正確並可容易使用。

問題與改善建議

I. 內容設計

觀察到的問題

The app lacks clarity in explaining the benefits of linking credit cards and distinguishing between My FamiPay and PlusPay. Moreover, the credit card validation fee of NT$ 1 is not disclosed upfront.

為什麼要關心這個問題？

For certain users, mobile payment may be a novel concept, and in Taiwan, where credit card details are considered highly confidential, a lack of transparency can potentially undermine users' confidence and trust, leaving them hesitant to try the service.

嚴重度指數：4/5

大多數的使用者會注意到此問題，並因此拖慢他們完成工作的速度。

The PlusPay and the My FamiPay are different services, but the app doesn't provide explanation.
Users will be charged NT$1 during the validation process.

觀察到的問題

Some content seems wordy and unclear. For example, the alert in Fig. 1 clarifies the buttons' functionality because their labels do not reflect the actual actions.

為什麼要關心這個問題？

Mobile users tend to glance at texts, so using succinct content is easier to digest and understand.

嚴重度指數：4/5

大多數的使用者會注意到此問題，並因此拖慢他們完成工作的速度。

II. 視覺設計

觀察到的問題

The app has several components that are not consistently presented throughout the app. For example, the passcode input UIs in Fig 2 are inconsistent.

為什麼要關心這個問題？

People tend to rely on their experience to respond and interact with the world. If the design is inconsistent, people have to put extra mental effort into learning and remembering. Additionally, design inconsistency also creates a negative impression as it looks unprofessional.

嚴重度指數：3/5

有些使用者會注意到問題，但問題並不影響操作。

Inconsistent top bars can be spotted in the app.
The passcode interface in the app is inconsistent.

觀察到的問題

Many interactive elements do not provide sufficient visual feedback upon touch.

為什麼要關心這個問題？

Touch feedback provides confirmation and highlights tappable areas for users to easily distinguish where their input has been received.

This is particularly useful when users' devices are not powerful.

III. 錯誤修正和預防

觀察到的問題

Users cannot proactively generate new barcodes on the main page (Fig 1) until the app directs them to the payment failed page (Fig 2).

為什麼要關心這個問題？

This may lead to a frustrating scenario where the barcode expires right as shoppers reach the checkout counter. Additionally, the "Back to Home" and "Retry" buttons on the payment failed page can be misinterpreted, causing confusion and potential navigation errors.

嚴重度指數：4/5

大多數的使用者會注意到此問題，並因此拖慢他們完成工作的速度。

Fig 1: The main page doesn't have a functionality for users to generate a new barcode.
Fig 2: Generating a new barcode is only possible once the current barcode has expired.

觀察到的問題

當使用者輸入錯誤的信用卡資料，APP只會改變文字欄位的顏色，而沒有提供任何文字解釋。

為什麼要關心這個問題？

只利用紅色來指示錯誤可能會讓色盲、色弱的使用者難以理解或注意到問題。另外，基於生活經驗，有些使用者需要詳細的引導才能知道自己該做什麼。

嚴重度指數：3/5

有些使用者會注意到問題，但問題並不影響操作。

The credit card numbers turn red when they are invalid, but no further instructions are provided.
There is no explanation for why the card expiration date field turns red.
