Is It Ugly UI’s Fault? Anything Else?

There was a problem with our OTA flow - Every time we release update package, the customer care team had to spend a lot of time to notify and educate users. But, with so much effort, our customer seemed still confusing, no matter they were new or old users. Except the ugly UI, I believe there was a underlying reason. So i drew a diagram of user journey to exam the OTA flow step by step.

Based on the flow, I found the OTA flow was lack of notifications. therefore, our customer had no way to know there is new update available.

sub heading goes here
What Did Customer Complain About?

On our product Facebook page, there were 3 frequently asked questions about system update:

  • What should i do to update my tablet?
    → Root cause: No notification
  • The update process went wrong, what should I do?
    → Root cause: No error handling and update instruction
  • How do i know my tablet is up to date?
    → Root cause: UI design
