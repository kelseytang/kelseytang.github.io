Design
Connectivity Takes the Lead

Babies are fragile and in need of 24/7 attention. New parents are busy and lack of sleep, so they seek gadgets to help them survive their multitasking life. It is easy to come up lots of ideas and put them into our product. But development resource is limited, we need to focus on the most important things.
