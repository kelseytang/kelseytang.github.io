Challenges
headlines

We had no metrics to analyze the previous design due to privacy laws in the US, which prevented us from tracking kids' tablet activities.

To portray the lives of modern American families, I drew on materials from the Pew Research Center and the Kaiser Family Foundation (KFF).

After analyzing these sources, I concluded that parents need a simple way to set up parental controls, rather than a complex tool like Google Analytics.

Parents have no time for Google-analytics-like tools

Parents have on 6 hours for their family
