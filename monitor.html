Design Considerations
Connectivity Takes the Lead

1. Adjusting the video quality automatically to adapt the connection

↑ To ensure an uninterrupted streaming, the system automatically adjust the video quality based on internet connection strength.

2. Providing pre-set device configurations to ensure a smooth experience

Watching videos on mobile may cost real money if users are not on Wi-Fi, so we turn off the following by default:

  1. Watch full HD 1080P
  2. Use mobile data

To prevent SD card damage or running out of memory too soon, we turn off the following features by default:

  1. Continuous video recording
  2. Record full HD 1080P

↑ We turn off many features by default to prevent unexpected incidents.
