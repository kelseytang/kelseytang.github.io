Redesign My FamiPay's User Flow

Here, I explored the posibility of communications.

About This Project

  1. All scenarios were from the Daily UX Writing
  2. The original challenge was to complete one scenario in one hour, but I extended it to 2 to 3 hours as I wanted the writing and the UI component to be coherent and fit the context

01 - The Payment Home
(No Credit Card)

Observation

  1. The tabs's touch feedback is unnoticeable and the visual is inconsistent with other pages.
  2. Unnecessary information to new users as the account hasn't been created.
  3. My FamiPay (Ref: A) and PlusPay (Ref: B) are different services, but there's no explanation.

Why this issue matters to users?

There is no way for non-registered users to try the app.
